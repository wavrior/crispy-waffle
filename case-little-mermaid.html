Overview

Full EMEA marketing campaign for The Walt Disney Company's theatrical release of The Little Mermaid across 15+ European markets. Working with a team of designers, I crafted promotional assets for Amazon and Fire TV — animated end-cards, product banners, and localised Disney+ advertisements strategically timed to the film's premiere.

Engaged partner brands including Lego and Mattel to co-promote their products within the Little Mermaid brand world, ensuring a cohesive campaign across digital, retail, and streaming platforms.

The work

The campaign spanned multiple digital platforms and formats — from full-screen animated end-cards to inline Fire TV banners — with every asset localised across the EMEA market footprint. The partner brand integrations required careful visual alignment between the Lego and Mattel product ranges and the Little Mermaid art direction, maintaining the Disney brand world across all co-promotional materials.
