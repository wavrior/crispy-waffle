About

REF 03 · Profile

Broadcast GFX editor and motion designer based in London, with 5+ years delivering promo packaging and campaigns for Warner Bros. Discovery, The Walt Disney Company and the Financial Times.

Day to day I work on HBO Max promo packaging at WBD — designing GFX, building After Effects toolkits with Essential Properties, and versioning campaigns across languages and formats. I care as much about the system behind a package as the frame the viewer sees.

MA in Virtual & Augmented Reality at Goldsmiths. Beyond broadcast, I prototype AR experiences, live-event lenses and interactive installations — and bring that real-time thinking back into promo work.
