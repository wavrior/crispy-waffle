About

Profile

Broadcast GFX editor and motion designer based in London, with 5+ years delivering promo packaging and campaigns for Warner Bros. Discovery, The Walt Disney Company and the Financial Times.

Day to day I work on HBO Max, Discovery, TLC, Cartoon Network promo packaging at WBD - designing GFX, building After Effects toolkits with Essential Properties, and versioning content across languages and formats. I care as much about the system behind a package as the frame the viewer sees.

MA in Virtual & Augmented Reality at Goldsmiths. Beyond broadcast, I develop AR experiences, live-event lenses and interactive installations, bringing that real-time thinking back into motion and promo work.

Always open to ambitious projects at the edge of technology and craft.
