Overview

EMEA Marketing Campaign for Disney's 2023 Christmas festivities, interwoven with the launch of the Wish movie. Collaborating with a team of designers and producers, I created diverse marketing assets across Amazon, Freevee, Fire TV, and Google tablet platforms — including a dynamic Fire Tablet animation used as a Google tablet background.

Multiple product carousels were designed and localised across the entire EMEA campaign, ensuring a cohesive and visually engaging brand experience throughout the holiday period.

The work

The campaign demanded flexibility across a wide range of formats and platforms — from the ambient motion of a Fire Tablet lock-screen animation to the structured product hierarchy of a multi-title Fire TV carousel. Each asset was produced within the Wish art direction and then localised across the EMEA market set.
